⚖️ Professional Bill & Tax Analyst (Reverse Calculator)A specialized Streamlit web application designed for accountants and administrators to reverse-calculate gross bill amounts based on required net profit and actual expenditures.This tool is specifically optimized for high-precision environments like hospital ward repairs or government contracting where taxes (GST, IT, BRA) and service commissions are deducted from the final cheque.🚀 Key FeaturesReverse Engineering Logic: Input your actual costs and target profit; the app calculates the exact Gross Bill you need to submit.Proportional Profit Allocation: Automatically hides the profit margin within the "Parts" and "Labor" line items based on their expenditure ratios.Detailed Tax Breakdown: Separates taxes for Parts (GST + IT) and Labor (BRA + IT) for 100% audit transparency.Audit-Ready PDF Reports: Generate and download professional PDF invoices with a timestamp and a full back-verification trail.Mobile-Friendly UI: Optimized for browsers on Android/iOS for on-site calculations.🧮 The Logic (Accountant's Formula)The application uses a nested "Gross-up" formula to ensure all deductions are covered:$$Gross = \frac{Net Required}{(1 - Tax_{effective}) \times (1 - Commission)}$$Where $Tax_{effective}$ is dynamically calculated based on the ratio of Parts vs. Labor expenditures.🛠️ Installation & Local SetupClone the repository:Bashgit clone https://github.com/your-username/your-repo-name.git
